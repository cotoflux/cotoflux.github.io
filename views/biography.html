Biography



I was born in Barcelona, but as a baby I got to travel the world. Professionally, I had the opportunity to work abroad and relocated twice to Paris. I also worked in Milan and London. I am used to working in an international environement.

After several experiences in the marketing world, I decided to continue my career creating my own business. While creating my own business I studied different disciplines and as my business grew I evolved from one shop to two and to a distribution business.

Unfortunatelly, afther years of owning my company I had to reinvent myself professionally and my resiliance has been tested, as I had to leave business. When I returned to Barcelona I discovered my new passion.

One day, late at night I aplied to Google Scholarship, was not sure if I could make it, as I had no experience in programming but with will and persistance I got my second scholarship and I realized how much I loved programming.

Since that day I decided to push further and further my skills to became a developer. I have been following several trainings that led me to consolidate my knowledge to became a Fullstack Software Developer.

Of course this a journey and I know that I will continue learning by working on projects like this one for instance. I am passionate about learning and developing the skills that businesses need.
